Who am I?

My background is in aerospace engineering at Politecnico di Milano with particular attention to the study of complex aerospace structures.
The use of computation tools always fascinated me and that brought me to jump from the aerospace field to the software development one. In the last years, I worked with machine learning tools and the Python development stack. Have you ever seen how fascinating is the process of a generative network? :)
Currently, I'm learning front-end and blockchain development.
Technology apart, I'm a second dan judo black belt who loves spending spare time playing padel with friends and reading books.
